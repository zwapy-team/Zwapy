Who We Are??

Zwapy is more than just a platform — it’s a movement built by students, for students.

We're a student-centric digital ecosystem designed to unlock the untapped potential within universities. Our mission is simple yet powerful: to empower students through skill exchange, collaborative projects, internships, freelance gigs, resource sharing, and event discovery — all in one place. We noticed something broken: while student talent is everywhere, the opportunities to apply it are scattered, siloed, or simply missing. Platforms like YouTube, LinkedIn, or freelance sites aren't built for the student journey — but Zwapy is.
